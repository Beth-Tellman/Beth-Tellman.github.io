Datasets

Mexico City

Data from my dissertation in Mexico City, including urbanization maps, electoral data, zoning changes, and land titling of informal settlments is freely available for download from UNAM. All metadata is currently in Spanish an English version is coming soon!

Flood Maps for Deep Learning

A dataset produced by Cloud to Street to train and test deep learning models for Sentinel-1 and 2 (Sen1Floods11) is freely available for download from The Cloud to Street Github.
