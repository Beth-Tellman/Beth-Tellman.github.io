Welcome

I am currently an Advanced Study Program post-doctoral fellow at the National Center for Atmospheric Research working with Clara Deser. My research is on understanding and modeling temperature variability and change. I am especially interested in connections between large-scale atmospheric variability and the observed surface climate. My most recent work is on changes in and causes of extreme temperature events, as well as links between natural variability and forced climate change.
