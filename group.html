Prospective post-docs

I do not currently have funding for post-docs, but if you are interested in collaborating on a postdoctoral fellowship, please be in touch. Some potential funding sources for post-docs are the NOAA Global Change post-doc, the various NSF post-docs, the McDonnell Foundation post-doc, and the Schmidt Science Fellows. In addition, the University of California system supports the President's Postdoctoral Fellowship Program for female and minority PhD recipients.

Prospective graduate students

Applications for graduate admissions to UCLA for the fall of 2019 have closed. In general, I am looking to take students through both the Institute of the Environment and Sustainability and the Department of Statistics PhD programs.
